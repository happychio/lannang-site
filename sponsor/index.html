Sponsor a learner

Keep Philippine Hokkien alive by gifting Lannang to the next generation.

Why this matters

Lannang-ue, the Hokkien of Chinese Filipino families, is fading with each generation. Many young Chinoys grew up hearing their ama and angkong speak it but never learned to answer back. You can change that for someone you love, or for a stranger who wants to reclaim the language of their family.

How sponsorship works

When you sponsor, you gift a full year of Lannang Plus, which unlocks every unit and unlimited hearts:

  • Gift it to someone you know. Sponsor your apo, your niece, your godchild, or a friend. They get a full year of Plus on their account.
  • Pay it forward. Sponsor a learner who wants to study but cannot afford it. We match your gift to someone on the waiting list.

Your name joins the Supporters wall below, honoring the parents, grandparents, and friends keeping the language alive, unless you prefer to give quietly.
