Learn the Hokkien your family speaks

Lannang teaches Philippine Hokkien (Lan-nang-ue), the heritage language of Chinese Filipino families, in bite-size lessons with audio, streaks, and a friendly guide named Dada.

See it in action Get the app

What is Lannang?

If you grew up hearing your ama and angkong speak Hokkien but never learned to answer back, this app is for you. Short, friendly lessons build from hello and thank you to real conversations, using the phrases Chinoy families actually use, including the natural mixing with Tagalog and English.

This is real Lannang-ue, not Mandarin and not textbook Taiwanese Hokkien. It is made for Gen Z Chinoys reclaiming the language of their families. The goal is confidence: enough vocabulary, phrases, and ear to start talking with the fluent speakers in your own home.

What you learn

  • Greetings, family, numbers, food, the market, feelings, home, and more
  • 22 units, from your first words to a full family-visit conversation
  • Real Lannang-ue, the way it is spoken at home and in Binondo

How it works

  • Bite-size lessons you can finish in a few minutes
  • Tap-to-build sentences with a mahjong-tile word bank
  • Audio on words and phrases, plus record-yourself practice
  • Streaks, XP, and a proficiency level that grows with you
