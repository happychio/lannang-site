What is Lannang?

If you grew up hearing your ama and angkong speak Hokkien but never learned to answer back, this app is for you. Short, friendly lessons build from hello and thank you to real conversations, using the phrases Chinoy families actually use, including the natural mixing with Tagalog and English.

This is real Lannang-ue, not Mandarin and not textbook Taiwanese Hokkien. It is made for Gen Z Chinoys reclaiming the language of their families. The goal is confidence: enough vocabulary and courage to say the next thing to the people you love.

Your guide

Meet Dada

Dada the chow chow cheers your wins, feels your misses, and keeps your streak alive. Get it right and you hear "Ho! Correct." Miss one and, well, your ama felt that. Dada believes in you. Chinchai lang.
